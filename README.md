The Stephen Weldon Gallery website was created to display the paintings and sculpture work of Stephen Weldon.  Stephen wanted a site for potential clients to inquire about the purchase or commissions of his work.  Stephen is a man in his 80s and prefers a simple interface system.  He requested that he would like to communicate with clients through email alone, with no e-commerce system for purchases.  Other than that I had free reign over the design and development of the site.  I created a Content Management System (CMS) site to give Weldon the ability to update his site  found here
